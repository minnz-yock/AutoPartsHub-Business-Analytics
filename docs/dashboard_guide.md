# Dashboard Guide

## Strategic Dashboard

Purpose:
Provides an overview of overall business performance.

Main KPIs:
- Total Sales
- Total Cost
- Total Profit
- Sales Forecast
- Top Customers

---

## Tactical Dashboard

Purpose:
Supports middle management by monitoring sales, inventory, and customer behavior.

Main KPIs:
- Monthly Stock
- Product Distribution
- Customer Segmentation
- Payment Methods

---

## Operational Dashboard

Purpose:
Supports daily operations by monitoring inventory and order activities.

Main KPIs:
- Stock Levels
- Shipping Performance
- Low Stock Warning
- Supplier Frequency
